Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

BetterHalf ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the BetterHalf iOS application.

Information We Collect

BetterHalf collects the following information, which you provide directly during setup:

  • Partner name — used to personalize gesture suggestions and reminders
  • Partner photo (optional) — used for display within the app
  • Relationship stage — used to tailor content to your situation
  • Important dates (birthday, anniversary) — used for reminders
  • Preferences — used to prioritize relevant gesture ideas

How We Store Your Data

All data is stored locally on your device only. BetterHalf does not have a server or backend. Your information never leaves your phone. We do not transmit, share, or upload any personal data to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Third-Party Services

BetterHalf does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service used is Apple's StoreKit for processing in-app subscriptions, which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with anyone. Period.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device for as long as the app is installed. You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset All Data" option in the app's Profile settings
  • Deleting the BetterHalf app from your device

Children's Privacy

BetterHalf is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
